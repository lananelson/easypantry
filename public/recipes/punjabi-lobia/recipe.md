## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon ghee or peanut oil

- 1 cup diced tomato
- 1/2 teaspoon ground turmeric
- 1/4 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper
- 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1/2 teaspoon ground coriander
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 cup dried black-eyed peas
- 2 cups water
- 4 cups raw spinach
