## Ingredients

- 2 cups olives (about 12-14 oz)
- 1 small bulb garlic (8-10 cloves, peeled)
- 6-8 anchovy fillets (about half a jar or 2 oz)
- 1/2 cup minced fresh parsley
- 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
- 2 tablespoons capers (optional)
- Zest and juice of 1 lemon
- Black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. In a food processor, combine the olives, garlic cloves, and anchovy fillets.
2. Pulse until roughly chopped.
3. Add the parsley, olive oil, capers (if using), and lemon zest and juice.
4. Pulse until the mixture reaches your desired consistency - some prefer it chunky, others smoother.
5. Season with black pepper to taste.
6. Transfer to a jar or container and refrigerate until ready to use.

## Notes

- Tapenade keeps well in the refrigerator for up to 2 weeks
- Great on toast, with eggs, mixed into pasta, or as a topping for grain bowls
- Adjust olive oil quantity for desired consistency
