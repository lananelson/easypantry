## Ingredients

- 1 loaf stale bastone (Italian bread), about 2 days old
- 2 tablespoons dried Sicilian oregano
- 2 tablespoons grated sharp pecorino (preferably Locatelli)
- 2 tablespoons garlic powder
- 2 tablespoons freshly ground black pepper
- 2 tablespoons onion powder

## Notes

- Breadcrumbs keep indefinitely in airtight containers in the freezer.
- No thawing needed before use; shake and use straight from frozen.
