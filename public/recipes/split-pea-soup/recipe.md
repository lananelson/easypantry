## Ingredients

- Olive oil
- Carrots
- Onion
- Celery
- Garlic
- Yellow split peas (soaked overnight)
- Chicken stock
- Water
- Smoked turkey (wing, drumstick, or other parts) or rotisserie chicken pieces
- Bay leaves
- Fresh thyme (optional)
- Crushed red pepper flakes
- Kosher salt
- Fresh parsley (for serving)
- Potatoes (optional)

## Instructions

1. Soak split peas overnight in water. Drain before using.

2. Sauté carrots, onion, and celery in olive oil in the Instant Pot (using sauté function) until softened. Add garlic.

3. Add drained split peas, chicken stock, water, smoked turkey or chicken pieces, bay leaves, thyme (if using), and red pepper flakes.

4. Slow cook on low for 1 hour until peas break down and soup thickens.

5. Remove turkey or chicken pieces, shred meat, return to soup. Discard bones and bay leaves.

6. Season with salt. Serve with fresh parsley.
