## Ingredients

- 1 cup dried chickpeas
- 2 to 3 bay leaves
- 3 cups water
- 1 tablespoon ghee
- 1/3 cup [onion masala](../onion-masala/recipe.md)
- 2 teaspoons chana masala powder
- 1 teaspoon salt
- Juice of 1 lemon
- 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
