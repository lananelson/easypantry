## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup short-grain rice
- 1/4 cup moong dal or other split dal
- 1 tablespoon ghee, plus more for serving
- 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
- 1/2 teaspoon ground turmeric
- 1/4 cup Spanish red or other roasted peanuts
- 1 3/4 cups water, plus more as needed
- 1 teaspoon salt
- Yogurt, for serving (optional)
