## Ingredients

- 2/3 cup ячменная крупа (cracked barley groats)
- 2 tablespoons butter
- Salt to taste
- 0.5 liter broth (Costco chicken bone broth works well)
- [Olive tapenade](../olive-tapenade/recipe.md) for serving

## Instructions

1. In the instant pot, bring the bone broth to a boil.
2. Add the ячменная крупа and a pinch of salt.
3. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 30 minutes until the grains are tender and have absorbed some of the liquid. Add water as needed to keep the grains submerged.
4. Stir in the butter until melted and well combined.
5. Serve hot, topped with a generous spoonful of olive tapenade.

## Notes

- This recipe can be made with other grains like buckwheat groats, pearled barley, or oats
- Adjust cooking time based on the grain used
- The savory tapenade pairs beautifully with the buttery, broth-cooked grain
