## Ingredients

- 1 cup heavy whipping cream, cold
- 2 tablespoons powdered sugar
- 1 tablespoon cognac
- 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)

## Instructions

1. Chill a large mixing bowl and whisk or beaters in the freezer for 10 minutes before starting.

2. Pour cold heavy cream into the chilled bowl.

3. Begin whipping on medium speed until cream starts to thicken.

4. Add powdered sugar, cognac, and vanilla extract (if using).

5. Continue whipping on medium-high speed until soft or medium peaks form, about 2-3 minutes total. Do not over-whip.

6. Serve immediately or refrigerate covered for up to 2 hours.

## Notes

- For stiffer peaks, whip a bit longer, but be careful not to over-whip or it will turn to butter.
- Can be made up to 2 hours ahead and kept chilled.
- Double the recipe for larger gatherings.
