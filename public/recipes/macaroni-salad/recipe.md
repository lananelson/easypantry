## Ingredients

- 1 lb macaroni (1 box)
- 2 medium carrots, grated
- 8 oz imitation crab, chopped (1 package)
- 2 tablespoons sesame oil
- 3 tablespoons rice vinegar
- 1 medium sweet onion, grated
- 1 bunch chives, chopped (about 1/4 cup)
- 1 orange bell pepper, diced
- 1 jar (12 oz) roasted red peppers, drained and diced (can use hot ones)
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- 1/4 cup milk

## Instructions

1. Cook macaroni according to package directions until al dente. Drain and rinse with cold water to cool.

2. In a large bowl, combine cooked macaroni, diced carrots, and chopped imitation crab.

3. Add diced sweet onion, chopped chives, diced orange bell pepper, and roasted red peppers.

4. In a small bowl, whisk together mayonnaise, milk, sesame oil, and rice vinegar until smooth.

5. Pour dressing over the macaroni mixture and toss to combine evenly.

6. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving to allow flavors to meld.
