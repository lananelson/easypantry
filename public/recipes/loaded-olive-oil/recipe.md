## Ingredients

- Extra virgin olive oil (enough to pool generously in a wide shallow dish)
- Balsamic vinegar, for drizzling
- Green olives, sliced
- Sun-dried tomatoes
- Fresh parsley, roughly chopped
- Grated parmesan
- Aleppo pepper or crushed red pepper
- Flaky salt
- Good bread, for dipping

## Instructions

1. Pour olive oil into a wide, shallow serving dish — enough to cover the bottom generously.
2. Drizzle balsamic vinegar in pools across the oil.
3. Add sliced olives and sun-dried tomatoes.
4. Pile chopped parsley in the center.
5. Snow parmesan over the top.
6. Finish with Aleppo or crushed red pepper and flaky salt.
7. Serve with bread.
