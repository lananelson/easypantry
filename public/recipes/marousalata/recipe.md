## Ingredients

- 1 head romaine lettuce, finely chopped
- 4-5 scallions, thinly sliced
- 1 large bunch fresh dill, finely chopped
- 1 small bunch fresh parsley, finely chopped
- 4 oz feta cheese, crumbled
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- 3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
- Juice of 1 lemon
- Zest of 1 lemon
- Salt to taste

## Instructions

1. Add oregano, lemon juice, lemon zest, and a pinch of salt to a small jar. Pour in olive oil, seal, and shake to combine.
2. In a large bowl, layer chopped romaine and scallions.
3. Top with dill and parsley.
4. Add crumbled feta.
5. Dress just before serving and toss to combine.
