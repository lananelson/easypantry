## Ingredients

- 1 everything bagel
- Cream cheese
- Sprouts
- Avocado
- Honey mustard
- Lettuce
- Swiss cheese

## Instructions

1. Slice the everything bagel in half.

2. Spread cream cheese on both halves of the bagel.

3. Layer sprouts, sliced avocado, honey mustard, lettuce, and Swiss cheese on the bottom half.

4. Top with the other half of the bagel and serve.
