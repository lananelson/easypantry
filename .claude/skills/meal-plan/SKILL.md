Start or update the weekly meal plan.

**Behavior:** If the user provided meal ideas or recipes, add them directly. Don't ask what they want to do — look at the current state of the plan and act on what they gave you. Confirm with the user only if it's late in the week and the target week is ambiguous.

Follow COMMANDS.md § "Meal Planning Workflow" for process, templates, and format rules.
